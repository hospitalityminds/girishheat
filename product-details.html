High Density
Cartridge Heaters

These are Made of Heating Conductor grade Ni-Cr 80:20 is evenly wound on a supporting mgo core, which is co centrally placed in a Chrome- Nickel Steel tube after which the inner vacant space is filled with specially selected high purity magnesium oxide powder. This assembled unit is then highly compressed in a SPM, thus converting the core, powder and conductor into one homogenous mass. The bottom end of the heater is welded with a disc washer for prevention against contamination.

Used in Packaging Industry, Marking and Sealing Machinery, Hot Runner Bushings, Medical and Laboratory Apparatus, Show Making Industry, Die and Platens, 3-D Printers.

  • Min Dia: 1 / 4" & 6mm / Max Dia: 1" & 32mm

  • Min length: 1½” & 40 mm / Mac length: 40" & 1000mm

  • Standard sizes available

  • Various watt density options available wiht in-built thermocouple optional

  • Highly non-corrosive with even heat profile and max temp upto 750°C

  • High purity magnesium oxide leading to superior life expectancy

  • Available with various Lead Exits and Cable Protection options

  • Sealed Bottom with Conical End disk to avoid contamination and for easy replacements

Coil Heaters

Girish-heat’ brand Coil heaters are designed and manufactured with advanced concept of thermal engineering which has a construction similar to high watt density cartridge heaters. These heaters are also known as high performance tubular heaters or cable heaters. The basic construction of these heaters consist of compacted MgO, high temperature resistance wire and Chrome Nickel Steel tube. These heaters can be constructed with or without built in thermocouples

Used in Packaging Industry, Marking and Sealing Machinery, Hot Runner Bushings, Medical and Laboratory Apparatus, Show Making Industry, Die and Platens

  • Standard sizes available with various cross section

  • Various Watt Density option available

  • Designed for even heat profile

  • Precision fit on Hot Runner Nozzles

  • Highly non-corrosive

Water Immersion Heaters
Commercial

These heaters are made of one Copper sheathed Tubular heating element filled with high grade insulation powder & Heating wire coil at the centre which is brazed to a Brass flange. Heaters are nickel chrome plated and are provided with earthing Terminals. A metallic cap is provided for protection of Terminals.

Used in Light duty (intermittent) boilers, Washing machines, Geysers, Coffee urns, Sterilizers and for other commercial purposes.
